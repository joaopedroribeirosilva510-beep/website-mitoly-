Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 09, 2026

Mitolyn Blog deeply values and respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains in a clear and transparent manner how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of data:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and other information you voluntarily provide when filling out forms or subscribing to our newsletter.
  • Usage and Browsing Data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, search terms, and general interactions with our content.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device used, date and time of access.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Files that store preferences, authentication, and analytical data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively for:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving our content about mitochondrial health and the benefits of Mitolyn;
  • Sending relevant communications, newsletters, scientific updates, and educational materials;
  • Personalizing your browsing experience according to your interests;
  • Performing statistical analysis to better understand our audience and improve the website;
  • Responding promptly to your questions, requests, and support;
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share information only in the following limited situations:

  • With trusted service providers (hosting, email delivery, data analytics, and marketing tools) who are contractually obligated to protect your data;
  • When required by law, court order, or competent authority;
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use essential, analytical, and functional cookies to provide the best possible experience. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time in your browser settings.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have full control over your data. In accordance with applicable laws, you may:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you;
  • Ask for correction, updating, or deletion of your data;
  • Withdraw consent for data processing;
  • Request portability of your data;
  • Opt out of receiving marketing communications.

To exercise any of these rights, simply contact us.

6. Data Security

We implement strict technical, administrative, and physical measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, loss, or destruction.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most recent version will always be published on this page with the updated date.
